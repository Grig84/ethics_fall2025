### USA
As mentioned in the overview, only 34 out of the 50 states have any legislation on use of autonomous vehicles. These laws are intended to restrict and regulate the use of level 4 and 5 autonomous vehicles, but generally these only regulate the use in general of autonomous vehicles, and have a very basic and limited safety standard that the vehicles have to adhere to. 
19 of the 34 states require a 'minimal risk condition', as mentioned previously, and that is (usually) their only safety requirement of the vehicle. This means that there is no standard for on-road behaviour or how accurate the autonomous systems are, and
